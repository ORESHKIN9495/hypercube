About Hypercube

“Hypercube Finance” is a derivative product of the TBCC developers,
which is the result of many years of painstaking work of the best
experts in artificial intelligence.

The neural network predicts changes in quotations with an accuracy of 70-80%
and allows you to determine the direction of changes in the price of an asset,
signaling the appearance of a point of entry into a transaction or an exit from it.
Thus, “Hypercube Finance” allows users to make money
on high-frequency trading.
