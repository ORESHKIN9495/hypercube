How it works?

At the moment, the service works exclusively on the referral system.
There are three types of wallets in the user's personal account:

Basic

Used to deposit and withdraw funds,
accrue passive income

Bonus

Used to deposit and withdraw funds,
accrue passive income

Staking

The amount of investment that
“works” for income.
You can only withdraw income
from this wallet, which on average
reaches 0.5% (from 0.1 to 1%)
per day.

In addition to direct income from investments, the wallet receives interest
from each replenishment of the staking wallet by referrals.
The top-up percentage depends on your status.
